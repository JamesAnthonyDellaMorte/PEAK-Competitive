ICON REQUIREMENTS FOR THUNDERSTORE
===================================

You need to create: icon.png

Specifications:
- Size: EXACTLY 256x256 pixels
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Location: Save to this folder (thunderstore/icon.png)

Design Ideas:
1. Mountain peak with checkered racing flag
2. Two colored teams (red vs blue) racing upward
3. Trophy with difficulty stars (★★★★★)
4. Minimalist geometric mountain
5. "PEAK" text with competitive theme

Quick Creation Options:

AI Generation:
  - ChatGPT/DALL-E: "Create a 256x256 gaming logo for a competitive mountain racing mod"
  - Midjourney: "/imagine 256x256 icon, mountain peak, checkered flag, gaming logo --ar 1:1"

Free Design Tools:
  - Canva: canva.com (use free templates)
  - GIMP: gimp.org (free Photoshop alternative)
  - Photopea: photopea.com (browser-based)

Stock Icons:
  - Flaticon: flaticon.com (search "mountain racing")
  - Icons8: icons8.com (free with attribution)

Once created, save as:
  thunderstore/icon.png

Then run:
  .\package-thunderstore.ps1

The script will automatically include it in the package!
